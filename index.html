Why do people choose Tradelize?

  • All exchanges in one place

    You can manage all your exchange accounts and trade from one place. The unified interface allows you to do it faster than on exchange platforms.

  • Complete transparent community

    All data on trades are transmitted via the API connection. The statistics are backed only by the trading activity on the trader’s exchange account.

  • Ecosystem with multiple interfaces

    You have access to your crypto via mobile, web, and desktop interfaces. These are three access points to the market that form a single trading ecosystem.

  • We do not have access to your funds

    We don’t touch your crypto. You keep your funds on your favorite exchanges. All deposits and withdrawals are made solely on your exchanges.

  • Customer oriented

    Our experienced staff and active traders can help clear up any problems, even those not connected to Tradelize products or the ecosystem

  • Your data is protected

    We use modern SHA-256 encryption algorithms. Entering APIs is protected by encryption and is shared only with the exchanges.
